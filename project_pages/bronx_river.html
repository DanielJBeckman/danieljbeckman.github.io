Bronx River iPad App

The Bronx River iPad App is a live environmental data visualization kiosk for the Bronx Zoo. Built for public education and scientific outreach, it displays real-time water quality data from the Bronx and Harlem Rivers. Developed and shipped as part of a USGS educational initiative.

Tech Used: Swift · iOS · Data Visualization · Environmental Sensors
