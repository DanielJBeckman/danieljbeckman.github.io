Aldara (D&D World Builder)

Aldara is a fantasy world builder app for Dungeons & Dragons players and dungeon masters. It features a beautifully hand-crafted map and an elegant interface for building lore, managing locations, and tracking campaigns. Still in active development, Aldara is a labor of love combining art, storytelling, and interactive design.

Tech Used: iOS · Swift · Fantasy Mapping · Interactive Design · WIP
