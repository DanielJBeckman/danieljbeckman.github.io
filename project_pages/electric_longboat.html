Electric Longboat

Electric Longboat is a stylized Viking rhythm game in VR where you row through thunderous waves powered by a metal soundtrack. The game combines Norse visuals, cartoon 3D art, and intense physical interaction in a surreal rowing mechanics. Built in Unity with custom shaders, silhouettes, and all-original artwork.

Tech Used: Unity · Shader Graph · VR SDK · 3D Animation · Stylized Art
