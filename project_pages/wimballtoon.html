Wimballtoon

Wimballtoon is a chaotic 80s-style multiplayer VR sports brawler where scoring a goal is only half the madness. Steal the ball with a monkey wrench, summon a humpback whale with Picard’s flute, or fly on a broomstick firing rocket launchers. Every match ends in absurd mayhem—announced with flair by the Florida Panthers’ radio voice.

Tech Used: Unity · VR Multiplayer · Physics · Animation · 80s Visual Design
