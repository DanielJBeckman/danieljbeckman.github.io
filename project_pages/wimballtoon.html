About the Game

Wimballtoon is a chaotic multiplayer VR sports brawler. Players score by tossing a ball into a massive goal — but sabotage is half the fun. Use a monkey wrench, summon a humpback whale with Picard’s flute, fly a broomstick, or fire a DOOM-style rocket launcher. Scoring is called by the iconic Florida Panthers’ radio announcer in full over-the-top style.

Tech Used: Unity · Multiplayer VR · Physics · Animation · Platform Testing

Platforms HTC Vive Focus 3 · Oculus Quest 2
